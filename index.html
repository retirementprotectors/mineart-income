The $217,000 Wells Fargo inheritance arriving next week covers the entire first-year premium for any option. This means Year 1 has zero impact on your retirement income — the GMIB continues its roll-up, and the JH policy is funded from money that didn't exist in your plan last month.

The Question Is Simple

The GMIB income is guaranteed for life. The JH policy converts that income into a tax-free legacy. How much of that income do you want to allocate to your heirs vs. keep in your pocket? All three options work. The inheritance funds Year 1 regardless. The difference is how much monthly income you keep after Year 1.
